With your trusty (bargain priced) starship at the helm and a head full of dreams, you aim to carve your path through the vastness of space, building your trading empire and leaving an indelible mark on the universe.

Whether you seek fortune, fame, or the elusive essence of truth itself, the infinite universe is yours to explore and conquer. The stars await your presence; it's time to forge your legacy among them!
